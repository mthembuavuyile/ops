The Product Logic

Five steps. Trigger to cash in bank.

Eliminate loose templates, lost email attachments, and manual payment tracking. Vylex Ops connects the dots.

01

Inquiry (Trigger)

Client requests changes via contact form, email, or chat. You trigger a new quote instantly.

02

Quote Compilation

Input items, quantities, and rates. The system generates unique transaction numbers.

03

Secure Share

Send the client a secure web link via WhatsApp or email. No PDFs to download or lose.

04

Instant Approval

Client clicks "Accept". The quote converts into an unpaid invoice automatically.

05

EFT Tracking

Monitor payments, view overdue logs, and reconcile amounts with simple manual confirmation.
